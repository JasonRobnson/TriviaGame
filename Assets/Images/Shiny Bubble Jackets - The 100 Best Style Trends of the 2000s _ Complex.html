39. Shiny Bubble Jackets

Unless you were a snowboarder, winter fashion usually focused more on function than style: Who cares if you look fly if you're freezing your ass off? But then companies like Moncler prettied up cold-weather outerwear by making fashionable jackets that didn't neglect the keep-you-warm part. Toasty!

LEADERS OF THE PACK:
